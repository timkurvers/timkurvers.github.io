Hi! I'm Tim and I build things.

Developer, open-source enthusiast, Dutch, living in Norway.

For the last decade I have mostly worked on web solutions ranging from small websites to full-scale experience centers, usually including a good dose of domain modelling, architecture, API design and actual frontend/backend development.

In my free time I love tinkering with the more obscure areas of our field — such as WebGL, networking and binary file formats — and occasionally apply these discoveries in coding challenges like Advent of Code.
